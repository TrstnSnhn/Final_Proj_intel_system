# Ethics Statement — PlantGuard AI

## Intended Use
PlantGuard AI is designed for preliminary plant disease screening only.

## Risks & Mitigations
- Misdiagnosis risk: display confidence and top-3 predictions.
- Dataset bias: report per-class metrics and highlight weak classes.
- Over-reliance: include explicit disclaimers and advise expert consultation.

## Data Privacy
PlantVillage contains no personal data.

## Fairness
Dataset is lab-curated and may not generalize to field conditions.

## Environmental Impact
Single-GPU short training setup intended to minimize compute footprint.
